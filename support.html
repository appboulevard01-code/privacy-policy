1. Getting Started

To begin using the app, follow these simple steps:

  • Google Sign-In: Upon launching the app for the first time, on the Welcome screen use the "Sign in with Google" button to authenticate yourself.
  • Once logged in, you will be redirected to the main Expense Overview screen.

This authentication method ensures your financial data remains secure and synchronized across devices.

2. Expense Overview Screen

The Dashboard is your financial control centre, designed to give you a quick snapshot of your financial health.

Key Features

  • Period Selector: Located below the title, this allows you to navigate through time. Use the Left (<) and Right (>) arrows to switch between previous or next periods.
  • Account Toggle: Manage multiple ledgers? Tap the account icon to switch views between accounts, such as Business or Savings. The default account (set in Settings) displays initially, but you can change it anytime.
  • Visual Summary:
    • Total Expense: The summary card displays the total account-specific expense for the selected period at the top left corner.
    • Pie Chart: A Pie Chart displays color-coded breakdown of where your money went by categories.
  • Budget Progress Bar: The progress bar visually represents your budget status for the selected time period.
    • Progress Bar Display Logic: The progress bar appears greyed out unless at least income is entered or a budget is set in Settings > Budget. If no budget is explicitly set, the entered income serves as the budget amount.
    • Green Bar: Represents your remaining budget.
    • Red Bar: Represents your total expenses.
  • Category wise total expense: The area below the pie chart lists each expense category along with its total amount. Clicking on any row displays all individual expenses recorded for that category within the period.

Interacting with Data

This drill-down feature allows quick access to detailed transaction views without navigating away from the overview. Use it to review specifics like dates, amounts, and notes for better financial insights.

3. Income Overview Screen

You can access Income Overview screen by swiping left on Expense overview screen. It provides a similar snapshot but focused on your income streams, showing you exactly where your earnings are coming from.

4. Managing Transactions

Logging your expenses and income is fast and intuitive. Tap the + Add Expense button on the Expense Overview screen or +Add Income button on Income Overview screen to begin.

Add Expense / Income Dialog

  • Date: Pre-filled with today's date. Tap to select a different date.
  • Amount & Calculator: Enter the amount using the custom number pad. Built-in Calculator: Perform math directly in the field (e.g., 50 + 20) and tap ✓ to calculate the total.
  • Category: Select a category from the popup grid to organize your spending.
  • Account: Choose which account allows this transaction (e.g., Personal). This defaults to your active account but can be changed per transaction.
  • Notes: (Optional) Add details like "Dinner with John" or "Office supplies". Notes provide context, making it easier to recall specific spending later and helping identify spending patterns during reviews.

5. Transaction History

The Transactions screen provides a comprehensive chronological list of all activity for a selected period and account. Tap the Transactions button on the overview screen to access it.

Finding Entries

  • Navigating Between Periods: Use the arrow buttons at the top to navigate between periods.
  • Search: Tap the magnifying glass to find transactions by Date, Category, Amount, or Notes.
  • Filter: Use the funnel icon to show only specific categories.
  • Sort: Organize your list by Date (Newest/Oldest), Amount (Highest/Lowest), or Category (A-Z/Z-A).

Editing & Deleting

  • Edit: Tap any transaction to open the Edit Dialog and make changes.
  • Delete: Simple swipe from right to left on any item to permanently delete it.

6. Setting Currency

Navigate to Settings > Currency to view a comprehensive list of countries and their respective currency symbols. Use the search bar to quickly find and select your preferred country, which will be updated throughout the app.

7. Setting Default View Period

Go to Settings > Default View Period to configure your default view period—Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly. This setting controls the overview screens, enabling you to track transactions on your preferred timeframe.

8. Managing Accounts

The app provides two default accounts—"Personal" and "Business"—with the active account highlighted. Navigate to Settings > Accounts to view, edit, or add new accounts.

Any single account can be set as the default. You can switch between accounts on the main screens by clicking the account toggle icon.

9. Budget Management

Take control of your spending limits by navigating to Settings > Budget.

  • Selecting an Account: Choose the specific account for which you want to set the budget.
  • Viewing Existing Budgets: See existing budgets or sort them for easy management.
  • Add Budget: Tap + Add Budget to create a new one. Enter the amount and select the period (Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly) and duration (single or recurring).

10. Carryover Budget

Enable the Carryover feature to automatically roll over unused funds to the next period.

Example: If your budget is $500 and you only spend $450, the remaining $50 is added to next month's budget, giving you $550.

11. Reminder and Notification

Configure reminders and notifications through Settings > Reminders & Notifications to stay proactive about your daily expense tracking. Both Budget Alert and Daily Reminder are enabled by default.

12. Set Budget Alert

Enable this to receive in-app alerts whenever your remaining budget falls below a threshold percentage you specify (e.g., 20%).

13. Set Daily Reminder

Receive daily notification reminders prompting you to log your expenses at your chosen time. Tap the time field to adjust it precisely.

14. Backup & Restore

Safeguard your data via Settings > Backup & Restore.

  • Google Drive Backup: Enable automatic backups directly to your Google Drive account for seamless syncing across devices.
  • On-Device Options: Create a local backup file stored securely on your phone's internal storage.
  • Backup & Share: Generate a shareable backup file to export via email or messaging apps.

Data Transfer Features

  • Export Data to Excel: Export your complete dataset to a structured Excel (.xlsx) file for advanced analysis.
  • Import Excel File: Upload a previously exported or compatible Excel file to bulk import data.

Restore Options

  • Restore from Device: Instantly recover your data from a local backup file.
  • Restore from Google Drive: Choose from your Drive backups to restore your data across devices.
