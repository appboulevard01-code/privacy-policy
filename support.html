Getting Started

Thank you for using our application. This guide will help you understand the basic functionality and how to use the app effectively.

After installing the application, create an account or sign in to access all available features.

Account Management

Users can manage their profile information directly within the app.

  • Update profile details
  • Manage account preferences
  • Access subscription information

Account Deletion

Users have the option to permanently delete their account and all associated data directly from within the application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the application.
  2. Navigate to Profile.
  3. Tap the Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated user data will be permanently deleted from our systems. This action cannot be undone.

Please note that deleting your account will also remove any active subscriptions associated with the account. If you choose to create a new account in the future, previously purchased subscriptions will not be restored and may need to be purchased again.

Manage or Change Privacy Consent

Users can review and modify their privacy consent preferences at any time through the application's settings.

How to Manage Your Privacy Consent

  1. Open the application.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll down and tap Privacy Settings.
  4. Update your consent preferences as needed.

Any changes made to your privacy preferences will take effect immediately.

Please note that the Privacy Settings option is only visible to users located in regions where privacy regulations such as the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) or the CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) apply.

Subscriptions

The application may offer premium features through subscription plans.

  • Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through the platform where the purchase was made.
  • Premium features remain active for the duration of the subscription period.
