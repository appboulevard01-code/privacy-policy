ExpenseWard Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

ExpenseWard is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using ExpenseWard, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

A. Personal Information (Account Data)

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Name and Email Address
  • Unique Google Identifiers
  • Profile Picture (if provided)

Purpose: To manage your premium subscription status and identify your account across devices. This data is stored securely in Google's Firebase Firestore.

B. Financial Transaction Data (Local Storage)

  • Nature of Data: All income, expenses, categories, notes, and budget entries you input.
  • Storage Location:
  • Our Access: We do not have access to your financial transactions.
  • Backups: If you enable optional Google Drive backup, a copy of your database is uploaded directly to your own private Google Drive account.

C. Sensitive Personal Information

  • Biometrics: App Lock feature uses device biometrics. We never collect biometric data.
  • Precise Location: We do not collect GPS data. Coarse location (City/Country) may be derived from IP address by advertising partners.

D. Media and Files

  • With your permission, the App may access device storage for database backup.
  • The App may access your gallery for profile picture selection.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We use third-party services to help operate and improve the app. These services may collect information such as device identifiers and IP addresses.

  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads and provide analytics.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to monitor app performance and engagement.
  • Google Play Services: Used for authentication and infrastructure.
  • Google Drive API: Used to backup and restore app database.

4. GDPR Compliance (For EEA Users)

  • Right to Access / Erasure: Users can view or delete their data from the Profile screen.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Users can manage ad preferences in the Privacy Options settings.
  • Legal Basis: Processing is necessary for subscription management and app improvement.

5. CCPA / CPRA Compliance (For California Residents)

  • Right to Know: Users can request categories of collected data.
  • Right to Delete: Account and Firestore data can be deleted via the Delete Account option.
  • Notice of Sharing: We do not sell personal data. Some identifiers may be shared with AdMob for advertising.
  • Opt-Out: Users may opt out of personalized ads from app settings.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until the app is removed or its data cleared.
  • Account Data: Stored in Firestore while the account is active. Deleting the account permanently removes associated data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.
